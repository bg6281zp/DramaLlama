How to upload a recipe

Once a brand new recipe has been uploaded...

  1. Enter the name of recipe in the upper left hand box (Where it says "Jumboliar" is pictured below)
  2. Type the action in the bigger box below(Where it says "mix" pictured below)
  3. The first box from left to right is quantity(Choose a fixed quantity)
  4. The second box from left to right is the units(Weight measurement)
  5. The third box from left to right is the ingredients(Stuff you put in your recipe)
  6. Click "Add Ingredient" at the bottom of the list!
  7. Click "Add Step" below that if there is more than just one step!
  8. Click "Save Recipe" and You're finished!
  9. Pat yourself on the back.

If however you click "Cancel/Edit" it will discard the recipe or edit the current recipe.
